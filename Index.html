My trading approach

HTF Analysis — Direction

I start on the higher time frames to determine the overall market direction and bias before doing anything else.

MTF and LTF — Key Levels

I then drop down to medium and lower time frames to identify strong support and resistance levels worth trading around.

Trade with the trend

I only take trades that align with the higher time frame direction. Patience and discipline — no counter-trend trades.

Technical and Fundamental

Chart structure is the foundation, but macroeconomic events and fundamentals are always part of the decision making.
